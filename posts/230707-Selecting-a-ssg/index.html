Selecting a SSG

I have been testing out several different SSG's and think I have ended up with Mkdocs. I started out with Hugo, gave Jekyll a quick attempt.

Both Hugo and Jekyll appear to be solid but the themeing is a bit too much for what i want to do. Mkdocs was a hassle to get set up as I wanted, but considerably easier than both Hugo and Jekyll.

The theme currently being used is readthedocs but I removed all the fonts and changed it to monospaced. Will update the about or licensed page with proper license.

Decided to ditch neocities in favor of GitHub, as I plan on making some stuff public.
